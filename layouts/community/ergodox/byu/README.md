 | TAB    |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |  [   |           |   ]  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  |   \    |

 | Esc    |   A  |   S  |   D  |LT 3,F|   G  |------|           |------|   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |   '    |
